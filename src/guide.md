## Nodes

Nodes is a claiming system seperated by territorys [see on dynmap](https://map.aechronis.net)
each node has its own reasorces:
- income (goes into /t income every hour)
- ores (when you mine stone ore has chance to go into inv) 
- crops (allows crops to grow in that node) 
- animals (allows animals to be bread in that region)

To join a town do /t join town name to apply or beg a friend to invite you. 
If you have no friends and no one is accepting you [see are discord](https://discord.aechronis.net) and find a group there I'm sure a group there would love free slaves (recruits).

### commands

- /town or /t for town commands
- /nation or /n for nation commands
- /nodes or /nd for node world infomation commands 
- /gc (general), /tc (town), /nc (nation) and /ac (alliance) to change chat channels.

### war

Declare war on a town or nation with /war enemyname
Attack enemy's territory by putting down a fence block in a chunk (fence is a "flag")

- You must attack from the outside of a territory
- Or attack from a captured chunk or ally territory
- The "flag" must see the sky (cannot place underground)
- Break the deepslate block on a flag to stop an enemy attack
- If the flag is not broken, after some time you will capture the chunk

Turn on minimap to see where to attack: use /t minimap
Capture a territory's home chunk ("H" symbol on the minimap) to capture the territory
