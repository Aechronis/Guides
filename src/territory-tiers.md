# Territory Tiers

---

## Nodesweeps

A nodesweep is the process by which staff evaluate territories submitted by nations. These evaluations assess build quality, quantity, and historical or thematic accuracy. Results are expressed as a tier on a scale of 1 to 10, with 1 representing the bare minimum of a town and 10 representing a flawless build. Tiers apply to individual territories only, even when a build spans multiple territories.

## What are the benefits of Territory Tiers?

Each territory tier gets an increased base income rate and mining rate. Higher territory tiers also unlock new buildings (see [Buildings](buildings.md))
